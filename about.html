Who is Dr. Biswajit Rath

A human-centric AI leader shaping the future with empathy & intelligence

Dr. Rath is a lifelong learner, mentor, and passionate advocate for making technology more human-centric. While his professional journey spans decades of experience, what truly defines him is his curiosity about people, ideas, and the evolving relationship between humans and intelligent machines.

Deeply fascinated by the potential of Artificial Intelligence, Dr. Rath has dedicated much of his time to helping professionals thoughtfully transition into the AI-driven world. Beyond formal roles, he finds immense fulfillment in coaching and mentoring individuals, guiding them not just in skills, but in developing the right mindset to thrive in a rapidly changing landscape.

He has been actively engaged in mentoring professionals through virtual platforms such as upGrad and continues to collaborate with leading academic institutions including IIT Mandi and TISS . For him, these engagements are not just about teaching technology, but about shaping responsible, aware, and future-ready individuals.

AI should enhance humanity, not replace it.

At the heart of Dr. Rath’s philosophy is a strong belief in humanising AI. He envisions a future where technology enhances human potential without losing sight of empathy, ethics, and societal well-being. His work consistently reflects this balance, bringing together innovation with a deep respect for human values.

Outside of his professional and mentoring commitments, Dr. Rath is a family-oriented individual who draws strength and inspiration from his close-knit family, his wife, two sons, parents, and extended family. He also cherishes a wide network of colleagues, students, and well-wishers, with whom he shares meaningful connections built over the years.

Whether he is mentoring a young professional, collaborating with academic institutions, or reflecting on the future of AI, Dr. Rath remains driven by a simple yet powerful purpose: to contribute to a world where technology serves humanity in the most thoughtful and meaningful way.
